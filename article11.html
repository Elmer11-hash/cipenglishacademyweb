"Free Shuttle Service"

At CIP ENGLISH, we provide a complimentary shuttle service for students after school and on weekends to make your stay more convenient and enjoyable.

•  Monday to Friday, 5:00 PM: Shuttle to the gym and Nepo Mall, located near the school.
•  Saturday, 1:00 PM: Shuttle to SM Clark for shopping and leisure.

Our vehicle service is also flexible and can be adjusted based on students' needs, ensuring your experience with us is both comfortable and convenient. Enjoy hassle-free transportation as part of our commitment to your comfort and convenience at CIP ENGLISH.

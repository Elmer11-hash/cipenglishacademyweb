"Mandatory Self-Study Penalty"

At CIP ENGLISH, we are committed to helping students achieve their academic goals.
To encourage accountability and consistent progress, a mandatory self-study policy has been implemented.
Students with poor attendance or those who do not demonstrate improvement in their English proficiency or test scores
may be required to complete mandatory self-study sessions.
This policy is designed to provide students with additional time to focus on their studies and reinforce their learning.
Our goal is to support all students in reaching their full potential.

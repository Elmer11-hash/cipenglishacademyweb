"2024 Yen-Campaign (Japan)"

This is a campaign for the Japanese market, which wants to study abroad but is burdened by the low value of the yen.
We hope that the fixed exchange rate of 130 yen per dollar will help alleviate the financial burden.
This campaign requires registration before December 31st and arrival at school before February 29th, 2025.

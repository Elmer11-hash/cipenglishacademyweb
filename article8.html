"Year-End and New Year’s Make-Up Class Schedule"

As the 2024-2025 year-end and New Year holidays approach, several national holidays may impact class schedules. To ensure our students continue to progress, CIP ENGLISH has arranged make-up classes for most holidays, with the exception of two days.

On December 24, 30, and 31, students will have:
•  2 hours of one-on-one classes
•  4 hours of group classes
Please note that there will be no classes on the following public holidays:
•  December 25 (Christmas Day)
•  January 1 (New Year’s Day)

We appreciate your understanding and remain committed to supporting your learning journey. For any questions regarding the make-up schedule, feel free to reach out to us.
